Bookmarking for Developers & Co

Bookmarks.dev is a bookmark manager created and designed primarily for developers and people who work in the information industry. We are also building the ultimate curated list of dev resources at https://github.com/CodepediaOrg/bookmarks. You can help by making worthy bookmarks public and rating existing ones. Check our HowTo page to get you started.

Bookmarking and managing bookmarks is made easy with the help of:

  • Bookmarklets - drag one of the links below to your browser toolbar
    • +Bookmarks.dev - popup - when clicked a popup to save the current URL is displayed [recommended for desktops and notebooks with good resolution]
    • +Bookmarks.dev - go back - when clicked it redirects you to the New Bookmark form. Once added you come back to the initial page [recommended for phones or smaller resolution devices]
  • Use the Save to bookmarks.dev chrome extension for Chromium based browsers - same behaviour as the first bookmarklet
  • Read Later - list of bookmarks to read later
  • Pinned - pin the bookmarks you intensively use currently
  • Favorites - add public bookmarks to favorites to find them in your personal searches
  • Save searches to use later for easier pin pointing known bookmarks

You can also choose only to browse our public repository of bookmarks. There are lots of good and curated resources from free programming books, to tech blogs covering most programming language and dev podcasts
